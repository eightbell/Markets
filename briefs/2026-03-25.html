Iran's 'non-hostile vessels' offer is the first crack in the Hormuz blockade but the ambiguous language means oil stays elevated. Day Ahead

  • All day - Iran 15-Point Plan Response - Trump sent the plan Tuesday. Tehran has not formally responded. The plan includes ending proxy support, full Hormuz reopening, and sanctions relief for civil nuclear cooperation. Every headline on Iran's response moves oil $2-3 and equities 0.5-1%. KEY
  • All day - Hormuz Transit Watch - Iran said 'non-hostile' vessels can pass. The market is watching whether any commercial tankers actually attempt transit today. A successful passage would be the strongest de-escalation signal yet. A blocked vessel would reverse the rally instantly. KEY
